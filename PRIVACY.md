# Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2026-01-01

Transportia ("App") is designed with privacy in mind. This policy explains what
information is (and is not) collected.

## 1. Data We Do Not Collect
- The App does not collect, store, or sell personal data.
- The App does not run analytics or advertising SDKs.

## 2. Local Device Storage
- The App stores user preferences and saved places locally on your device.
- This data remains on your device unless you delete the App or clear its data.

## 3. Third-Party APIs
- The App may call third-party APIs to retrieve transit information.
- These providers may receive data necessary to fulfill requests (such as
  search queries or location coordinates).
- We do not control how third parties process data. Please review their privacy
  policies before use.

## 4. Location
- If you enable location features, the App may use your device location to show
  nearby transit information.
- Location is processed on your device and may be sent to third-party providers
  only as needed to fetch results.
